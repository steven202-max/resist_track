How to Get Your IDs
Patient ID

Your Patient ID is a unique number assigned when you were first registered in our system. It can be found on your medical records or prescription documents.

Prescription ID

Your Prescription ID is generated when a prescription is created. It's usually printed on your prescription slip or mentioned by your doctor.

Can't find your IDs?

Please contact your doctor or the medical facility where you received your prescription. They will be able to provide you with the correct IDs.
